Patna

Patna, the capital city of Bihar, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited places in the world. Formerly known as Pataliputra, it has been a hub of power, learning, and culture since ancient times. The city was home to great dynasties such as the Mauryas and the Guptas, making it an important center of history. Today, Patna offers a blend of historical landmarks, cultural sites, and modern urban life, attracting tourists, pilgrims, and scholars alike.

You Should Explore in Patna:

  • Climb to the top of Golghar for a breathtaking city view.
  • Explore Patna Museum's rich collection of Mauryan and Gupta artifacts.
  • Visit Takht Sri Patna Sahib, one of the holiest places in Sikhism.
  • Experience peace and meditation at Buddha Smriti Park.
  • Walk around the historic Gandhi Maidan.
  • Discover the mysteries of Agam Kuan, an ancient well with legendary significance.
